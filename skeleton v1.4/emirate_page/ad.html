HISTORY OF ABU DHABI

Abu Dhabi’s early history resembles nomad settlements with typical herding and fishing lifestyles. Having thoroughly developed from trading pearls to exporting oil. The emirate’s long term investment in the oil industry paved the way to key tourism developments, such as Saadiyat Island and Al Grum Resort, attracting visitors from all over the globe.
