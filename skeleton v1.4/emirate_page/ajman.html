HISTORY OF AJMAN

Akin to other emirates, the Ajman region and its settlers dabbled on fishing and pearling and as the region’s biggest boat building center, with thousands of dhows that sailed through the Gulf constructed in its shipyards—through skills and craft passed down through tradition.

After its contentions with the British Naval Powers and truces and peace treaties ensued, the emirate found itself in federation with others hence the birth of the United Arab Emirates.

Explore

Being in the middle of the Emirates, it enjoys easy accessibility. Known for its beaches where you can spot dolphins to its souqs and cultural spots. Ajman, despite its size, promises a worthwhile experience on a budget.
