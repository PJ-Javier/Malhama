Lamer Beach

Bring the whole family to La Mer's beach and enjoy a mini break without boarding a flight. Rent a cabana with views of the Arabian Gulf, sun yourself on our comfortable daybeds while your little ones build a sand castle, take a cooling dip in the sea, or try your hand at paddleboarding, surfing or a range of other water sports. From water slides to water sports, movies to inflatable playgrounds, beach strolls to swims, gourmet dining to high-end shopping, the whole family can get in on the action at La Mer.
