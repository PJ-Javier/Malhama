Burj Al Arab

Burj Al Arab Jumeirah is undoubtably one of the most famous landmarks and tourist attractions in Dubai, and is renowned for its 'seven-star status'. The luxury all-suite hotel includes a range of fine-dining restaurants, incredible beaches and the expansive Burj Al Arab Terrace. Since its opening in 1999, the Burj Al Arab has played host to a covetable guest list of VIPs and celebrities from all over the world, helping put Dubai on the global luxury tourism map. Built on its own island, the sail-shaped building rises 321m, providing guests an amazing hotel room view of the Arabian Gulf.
