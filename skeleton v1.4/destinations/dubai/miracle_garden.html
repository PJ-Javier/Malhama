Miracle Garden

Promising a full bloom in fun and celebrations for the upcoming months, Dubai Miracle Garden - the world's largest natural flower garden - has a variety of new and exciting attractions and activities each season for the people of all ages. Dubai Miracle Garden has been designed to be a verdant treasure trove of experiences that capture the imagination, thanks to the breathtaking landscaping and a range of experiences you can share with friends and family. Among the attractions are the new Smurfs village, located in the largest natural flower garden in the Miracle Garden. There is also entertainment and dining available, such as the Floral Parade, play zones for the children, family Zumba, restaurants, cafes, etc.
