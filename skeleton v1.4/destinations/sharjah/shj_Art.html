Sharjah Art Museum

The Sharjah Art Museum is an art museum in the city of Sharjah, United Arab Emirates. Constructed in 1995 and inaugurated in 1997, it is housed in Bait Al Serkal in Al Shuwaihean Area. The building itself consists of two wings, interconnected by two passageways over an interior street. It is one of the leading art institutions in the Arabian Gulf region. It also organizes and hosts temporary exhibitions and educational events. Until 2023, it will host a selection called A Century in Flux, consisting of key modernist paintings, sculptures, and mixed media artworks from the Barjeel Art Foundation. The museum houses a collection of art from contemporary and modern artists from the UAE and other Middle Eastern countries. There are also temporary exhibits, curated and in collaboration with other institutions. Much like the Sharjah Museum of Islamic Civilization, there are different types of booking available from the sharjahmuseums.ae website, from family and educational visits, and programs.
