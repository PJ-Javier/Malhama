Sharjah Museum of Islamic Civilization

The Sharjah Museum of Islamic Civilization is a museum in Sharjah, United Arab Emirates. This museum covers Islamic culture with more than 5,000 Islamic artifacts, consisting of calligraphy, carvings, ceramics, coins, etc. on display. The museum is located on Corniche Street in the Al Majarrah area. The artifacts, collected globally, are displayed in seven galleries, six of which house permanent exhibitions. The seventh gallery is dedicated to temporary exhibits, with a roster of co-curated displays from other museums. There are also facilities for prayer, refreshments, souvenirs, parking, and rest rooms. There is also wheelchair access for persons with disabilities. Group visits with guides, free of charge, are available for booking from the sharjahmuseums.ae website, as well as academic visits. There are also special educational programs and workshops. Lastly, a sightseeing bus tour is also an option.
