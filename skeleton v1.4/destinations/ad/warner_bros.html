Warner Brothers World

The world’s biggest and fully air-conditioned indoor theme park, Warner Bros. World Abu Dhabi brings all the legendary characters from this famous studio to life. Meet Superman, catch up with Batman or Wonder Woman, or be entertained by Bugs Bunny, Scooby-Doo, Fred Flintstone and more, all within an impressive 1.65-million-square-foot theme park boasting 29 state-of-the-art rides, interactive family-friendly attractions, upbeat live entertainment, a range of dining options to suit all tastes, and exclusive shopping outlets.

This nostalgic experience starts as soon as you step beyond the Warner Bros. shield, where you are transported to six magical and immersive worlds made up of action, adventure and eccentricity. Dive deep into Bedrock, Dynamite Gulch, Cartoon Junction, Gotham City, Metropolis and Warner Bros. Plaza, with each of these magical lands featuring its own detailed scenic backdrops and authentically themed restaurants. Every attraction is fashioned exactly to the style of Warner Bros.’ key sets, making you feel as though you’re really part of the adventure.
