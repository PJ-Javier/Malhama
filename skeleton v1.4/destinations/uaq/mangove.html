Mangrove Beach

Located at Khor Al Yeefrah, this beach is the first of its kind in the country, promoting eco-tourism by giving importance to the beach's prominent mangroves, which protect the shore from storms and rising sea levels. The mangrove forests, also housing several species of amphibians, plants and birds, are available to be explored at your leisure. The rest of the beach is also perfect for camping, thanks to the calm waters, low waves, and soft sands. There are playground equipment for the children and restaurants nearby, with more public facilities under construction by the local government. As this beach is currently still under development as an eco-tourist location, there are definitely more attractions on the way. This beach is quite near to the Kite Beach Centre. Entry fee is 20 AED.
