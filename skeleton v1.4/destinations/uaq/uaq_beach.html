UAQ Kite Beach

Self-explanatory, the Umm Al Quwain Beach is less-crowded, perfect for those who want a more peaceful and calm experience or larger gatherings. The main beach of Umm Al Quwain follows along the western rim of the emirate. The usual amenities are present; spots for relaxation, walks, exercise, and barbeque sessions. There are also resorts and hotels located here, well-known by Dubai residents and locals alike looking for a cheap beach getaway, for the good value of their room rates. A private section of the beach, Kite Beach Center, contains facilities that further enhance your beach experience, such as an excellent restaurant. Back to the main beach, there are also watersports available for the sportier types, as kayaks, paddleboards. and kitesurfing equipment available for rent. In fact, Kite Beach Center also organizes guided kayak and paddleboard tours of the emirate's mangrove forest.
