SAQR PARK

Saqr Park is an expansive community parkland in Ras Al Khaimah. It hosts a number of activities that are for almost everyone, and the park itself is available all year round. It was inaugurated in 1991. It features the usual park amenities, including sports and play areas like skateboard parks, bike trails, arcades, a table tennis court, and some shops. There are also rides that are fun for the kids, with bumper cars, a merry-go-round, carousel, splash ride, Ferris wheel, and a rollercoaster operating in the park. Of course, there are also family areas such as picnic and barbeque spots, not to mention scenic areas like the date palm tree-garden or the park itself, perfect for just general relaxation or for a board game or two.
