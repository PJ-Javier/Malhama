KHATT SPRINGS

An oasis famous for its hot springs of sulfuric water, it's free for the public. Located 20 kilometers from RAK city and located right next to the Hajar Mountains. The springs, with a castle-like structure built upon it, are surrounded by a lush oasis, majestic mountains, and palm trees. It offers a number of opportunities for relaxation such as hot and precious stone treatments, therapeutic hot springs, and Ayurvedic treatments. These (along with the private pools, treatment rooms and cafes) are available at full access for visitors. The water is stated to have medical benefits for rheumatic, skin and muscle ailments, owing to its mineral content. The areas surrounding Khatt also feature about 170 archaeological sites.
