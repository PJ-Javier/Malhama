Khor Fakkan Beach

Located on the UAE's eastern coast, while this spot is technically an exclave of Sharjah, it is geographically surrounded by Fujairah. The beach hosts a variety of water-related activities like fishing, diving, parasailing, and kayaking. You can explore the waters by swimming, rented boat, or jetski. Further into the water there are spots for fishing, and the reachable-by-boat-ride Shark Island has snorkelling, scuba diving and sailing activities available. If water activities aren't your thing, there are playgrounds, and football goalposts on the sand. The Oceanic Resort and Spa is located here on one end of the beach, offering a tailored hotel experience, with many tourists choosing to spend the night here to rest.
