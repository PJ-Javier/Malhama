KALBA CORNICHE PARK

Kalba Corniche Park, located on the Sharjah-Fujairah border, is one of the places in Fujairah that's perfect for a relaxing time with the family or the friends. Quite popular among the locals and tourists alike, the place lends itself well to parties, picnics or just general recreation or relaxation. There are several spots available for a barbecue grill session, as well as a trail for a jog or a stroll. There is also a beach nearby (Kalba Beach) if you're feeling up for a walk on the sand or some fun in the water. Finally, there are also a couple of restaurants nearby.
