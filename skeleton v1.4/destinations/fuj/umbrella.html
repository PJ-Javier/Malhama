UMBRELLA BEACH

Umbrella Beach is a newly developed seafront complex consisting of, restaurants, coffee shops, food trucks, and more. The project intends to establish a commercial landmark in the Emirate of Fujairah, bringing benefits to business owners, customers, as well as the local population. Not only is it limited to those kinds of outlets, it has also been designed and built with a variety of attractions and activities. There are recreational spaces for children and adults, including playgrounds, trails for jogging, sports courts, and water activities. According to the description of the location's website, Umbrella Beach is on track to becoming the number one destination for families and young demographics on Fujairah's east coast, drawing crowds from Fujairah itself, Kalba, Khor, Fakkan and Dibba. It is also available for reserve for activities and events, including birthdays, weddings, corporate events etc.
