Corniche Al Fujairah

Stretching out from Kalba to Khor Fakkan, the Fujairah Corniche road is a very popular tourist spot in Fujairah. Set on the coast of Gulf of Oman, it is a seafront development in Fujairah City, and with it comes the popularity of seafront and mountain views, along with pristine beaches, cafes, restaurants, etc. along the road this spot is located on. Some of the best malls in Fujairah are located here, as well as schools, religious places, and medical facilities. Back to the Corniche itself, there are plenty of places for recreational activities, with parks and playgrounds, as the Kalba Corniche Park is located here too.
