Al Bithnah Fort

Another historical location, Al Bithnah Fort is located in the Wadi (valley) Ham, near Bithnah in Fujairah. A significant part of the UAE's history was contributed to by this fort, particularly in the Fujairah emerging as an independent emirate in the 20th century. The Wadi Ham being overlooked by the fort's position, Al Bithnah replaced an Iron Age fortification. It was the site of many conflicts between the tribes of the UAE (and eventually the British) in it's history, from it's construction in 1745 to until Fujairah's independence in 1939. Bithnah Fort itself is a rectangular structure with two round corner towers, built by stone, wood, and mud-based bricks and mortar.
