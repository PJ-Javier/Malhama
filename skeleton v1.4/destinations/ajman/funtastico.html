Funtastico

Funtastico's outdoor space is dedicated to bring fun for the young ones, friends and the entire family through classic rides and attractions.   Children can spend their time on a number of timeless games such as Bumper Boats, the Ball Pit and a collection of the coolest Bouncy Castles and Slides. Other adrenaline-inducing games such as the Super Jumper and Trampoline with Airbag can be enjoyed by both children and adults.   Their bike rental zone has exciting options for all ages where groups can cycle together and enjoy a cruise on the Al Zorah Marina shoreline.
