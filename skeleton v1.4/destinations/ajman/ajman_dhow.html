Ajman Dhow Yard

Ajman Dhow Yard UAE is a unique tourist attraction in Ajman. It offers a rare glimpse into the ancient craft of dhow making. A dhow is a wooden boat with one or more sails. The dhow sails are called “lateens”. Each lateen is handmade and the fabric is carefully woven to provide speed and agility. The dhow building techniques have passed down from generation to generation. The construction process has not changed over the years. The most surprising part of the construction process is that no written manuals or blueprints are used during dhow construction in Ajman. The dhows made in Ajman are known for their sturdiness and premium quality. From showing different stages of dhow construction to the Al Gaffal Dhow Race, the Ajman Dhow Building Yard preserves the rich history and significance of the vessel in the UAE.
